Algorithm Parameters

Fine-tune the detection sensitivity

r
1 – 20 Default: 4

Higher values model more complex signals but increase computation time.

η
0.5 – 10 Default: 3.5

Lower = more sensitive (detects more clicks). Higher = more conservative.

λ
0.9 – 0.999 Default: 0.99

Controls adaptation speed. Closer to 1 = slower but more stable adaptation.
